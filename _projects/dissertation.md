My doctoral dissertation examines how **inequality perceptions** shape **system justification** and **redistributive attitudes** in Chile, using experimental methods and causal inference.

### Methods
- Survey experiment with nationally representative sample
- Causal forest analysis for heterogeneous treatment effects
- Mediation analysis (emotional pathways)
- Pre-registered confirmatory and exploratory analyses

### Status
Results expected 2026. Thesis structured as three-paper format.
