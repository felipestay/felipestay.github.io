An **IDRC-funded** scoping review examining policies, services, and lived experiences of street-involved youth across Latin America. The review synthesises **193 studies** from 7 country case studies (Chile, Mexico, Brazil, Colombia, Argentina, Peru, Ecuador).

### Scope
- 193 studies across 7 countries
- Mixed-methods integrated approach
- Policy and intervention analysis

### Dissemination
- CCGH Halifax 2025 (conference presentation)
- FEANTSA 2026, Lisbon (accepted abstract)
