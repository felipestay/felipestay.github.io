Building a **press-based mortality registry** for people experiencing homelessness in Chile (2020–2025), in the absence of official records. Collaborative project with Alejandro Del Río.

### Findings
- Violence, not cold exposure, is the leading cause of death
- Geographic concentration in Santiago and Valparaíso
- Premature mortality patterns across causes

### Output
- IJoH 2025: *Presumed Deaths Among People Experiencing Street Homelessness in Chile*
- El Mostrador opinion column (October 2025)
