Publications

Personas en situación de calle: desafío pendiente para la política social en Chile

This article discusses the challenges of addressing the issue of homelessness in Chile. The article was published in the journal Persona y Sociedad/UAH.

Estudio Levantamiento de Experiencias Internacionales de Reconversión Residencial de Protección Especializada

This study reviewed international experiences in the conversion of residential protection facilities. The study was conducted by the Ministry of Social Development and the Inter-American Development Bank.

Reflexiones sobre el Programa Abriendo Caminos: Notas para la política social

This book of essays reflects on the Abriendo Caminos program, a Chilean government program that provides support to children of incarcerated parents. The essays were written by a variety of authors, including academics, policy makers, and practitioners.

The book is available for purchase from the Editorial Universitaria website.

Apoyo para el Fortalecimiento de Competencias Sociales para niños de 0 a 7 años. Manual de Apoyo para el Trabajo

This manual provides guidance on how to strengthen social skills in children from 0 to 7 years old. The manual was developed by the Ministry of Social Development and the University of Chile.

The manual is available for free download from the MIDES website.

Apoyo para el Trabajo con Niños Vulnerables. Set de cuentos para niños

This set of children's stories was developed to support work with vulnerable children. The stories were written by a variety of authors, including Chilean authors and international authors.

The set of stories is available for free download from the MIDES website.
